ABOUT ME

Welcome to the imaginative world of Shanuka Dilshan! At 23, I bring vibrant characters and mesmerizing 3D creations to life, merging fantasy with reality through my digital artistry. As a passionate cartoon character designer and 3D artist, my journey began with a pencil and a dream, evolving into a dynamic career where each project is a new adventure. I strive to infuse personality and soul into every character I create, crafting stories that resonate and visuals that captivate. Join me on this artistic voyage, where creativity knows no bounds and every pixel tells a tale.

MY SKILLS

Character Conceptualization

I excel at creating unique and memorable characters, bringing initial sketches to life with detailed designs that resonate with both the story and audience.

3D Modeling and Sculpting

Specializing in transforming 2D concepts into detailed 3D models, I ensure each character is visually appealing and technically sound for animation or display.

Texturing and Shading

My texturing and shading skills add depth and realism to 3D models, using vibrant colors and nuanced details to create dynamic, expressive final renders.

MY EDUCATION

Higher Education Diploma in Character Designing and Sketching Cartoon Characters

This diploma provided me with specialized training in the art of character design and cartoon sketching. Throughout the program, I immersed myself in the principles of character creation, learning how to develop unique and engaging personalities from initial concept sketches to final digital renderings. The curriculum covered advanced drawing techniques, anatomy, and movement, enabling me to create dynamic and expressive characters. Additionally, I gained proficiency in using industry-standard software for digital art and animation, further enhancing my ability to bring characters to life in 3D. This comprehensive education has equipped me with the skills and knowledge to excel in the competitive field of character design and animation.
